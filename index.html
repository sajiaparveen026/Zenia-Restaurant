About Zenia Restaurant - Food and Cafe

Founded with a passion for gastronomy and a vision to create a unique dining space, Zenia began as a dream to bring together the finest ingredients, innovative recipes, and a cozy ambiance. Our journey started with the simple idea that food should be more than just a meal—it should be an experience. Today, Zenia stands as a testament to that vision, offering a diverse menu that caters to every palate.

Our Menu

At Zenia, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our menu is a carefully curated collection of both classic favorites and contemporary creations, each crafted with the freshest ingredients and a touch of love. From hearty breakfast options to sumptuous dinners, and from delightful desserts to aromatic coffees, every dish is designed to delight your senses.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing exceptional service and unforgettable food experiences. Our chefs are dedicated to culinary excellence, using only the highest quality ingredients. We prioritize sustainability and local sourcing to ensure that every meal is not only delicious but also responsibly prepared.

Steven Muncaster

(Our Founder)

Steven Muncaster, founder of Zenia Restaurant, is a renowned chef and entrepreneur with over two decades of experience. A graduate of the Culinary Institute of America, Steven has worked in some of the world's finest kitchens. His journey from his grandmother's kitchen to becoming a culinary visionary inspired him to create Zenia, where exceptional cuisine and warm hospitality meet. Under his leadership, Zenia has become a beloved destination for food enthusiasts and casual diners alike.

Thank you for choosing Zenia. We look forward to serving you and making your dining experience truly special.
